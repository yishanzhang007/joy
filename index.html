ABOUT JOY
INSPIRATION AND MORE

Is a serif typeface inspired by classic roman font. I was always fascinated by the juxtaposition of font that has sharp shapes but somehow feels gentle and elegant. This is my first attempt at designing my own, and it’s the journey that brings me joy - hence the name.
